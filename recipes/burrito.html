Rick's Big Bad Bean Burrito

Description

A bean burrito typically consists of a flour tortilla filled with refried beans, cheese, and various toppings such as lettuce, tomato, and salsa. Avocado can also be added to the burrito for added flavor and nutrition. The combination of beans and avocado makes for a delicious and filling meal that is often enjoyed as a quick and easy lunch or dinner option.

Bean burritos are a popular Mexican dish that has become a staple in many parts of the world. They are often served with rice and a side of guacamole or salsa. The burrito can be customized to suit individual preferences, with additional toppings such as sour cream, jalapenos, or cilantro. Some people also like to add meat to their burrito, such as grilled chicken or beef. Overall, bean burritos are a tasty and satisfying meal that can be enjoyed by vegetarians and meat-eaters alike.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup vegetarian refried beans
  • 1 large whole-wheat tortilla
  • 2 romaine lettuce leaves
  • ½ avocado, peeled and sliced
  • ¼ cup fresh pico de gallo

Steps

  1. Stir refried beans in a saucepan over medium-low heat until hot, 2 to 4 minutes.
  2. Warm tortilla in a skillet over low heat until softened, 1 to 2 minutes. Transfer tortilla to a plate.
  3. Layer lettuce onto tortilla. Spread warmed refried beans, avocado slices, and pico de gallo on top of lettuce. Roll tortilla around the fillings into a vurrito shape.
