Artichoke and Sun-Dried Tomato Chicken<

Description

Artichoke and Sun-Dried Tomato Chicken is a flavorful and hearty dish that is perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion. The combination of tender chicken, tangy artichokes, and sweet sun-dried tomatoes creates a delicious and satisfying meal that is sure to please everyone. This dish is easy to prepare and can be customized to suit your taste preferences.

To make Artichoke and Sun-Dried Tomato Chicken, you will need boneless, skinless chicken breasts, canned or frozen artichoke hearts, sun-dried tomatoes, garlic, chicken broth, and a few simple seasonings. The chicken is first seared in a skillet until golden brown, then the artichokes, sun-dried tomatoes, garlic, and broth are added to the pan. The mixture is then simmered until the chicken is cooked through and the flavors have melded together. This dish can be served over rice or pasta, or with a side of roasted vegetables for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green peppers and onions
  • Â¼ cup sun-dried tomato pesto
  • 1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts in water, drained and quartered

Steps

  1. Season both sides of chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Place chicken in skillet; cook, turning once to brown each side. Remove chicken from pan, and set aside.
  2. Pour tomatoes into pan; cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly, and incorporating any brown bits from bottom of pan. Stir in pesto and artichokes, and return chicken to pan. Cover, and reduce heat to medium. Simmer for 5 to 10 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.
